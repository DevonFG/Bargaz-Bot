Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information this Discord bot collects, how it is used, how it may be stored, and what rights users have regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

This bot may collect and store information necessary for functionality, including but not limited to Discord identifiers, third-party platform identifiers, server configuration data, user-provided inputs, and operational logging data.

This may include information provided directly by users, information generated through bot interactions, and information retrieved through supported third-party platform integrations such as YouTube and Twitch.

2. How Information is Used

Collected information may be used to provide, maintain, and improve the bot's functionality.

This includes, but is not limited to, sending automated notifications, tracking content updates from supported third-party platforms, storing server configurations, processing user commands and inputs, logging events for functionality purposes, and maintaining operational stability through debugging and system maintenance.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Data collected by the bot may be stored for as long as necessary to provide functionality, maintain server configurations, support integrations, and operate bot features.

Stored data may be removed automatically by system processes, manually by the developer, or upon request of the user. Data may also be deleted when the bot is removed from a server or when associated features are disabled.

4. Access to Stored Data

Stored data may be accessed only by the developer and authorized automated system processes for the purposes of maintaining functionality, debugging issues, managing configurations, and supporting bot operations.

No other users or third parties have direct access to stored data unless explicitly provided through bot-generated outputs or configured features.

5. Data Sharing

Stored data is not sold, rented, or intentionally shared with third parties.

Limited information necessary for bot operation may be transmitted to support third-party services as required for bot functionality, including but not limited to platform integrations, API requests, notifications, and automated operations.

6. Third-Party Services

This bot may interact with and rely on third-party services, platforms, and APIs, including but not limited to YouTube, Twitch, and Wix.

These services operate independently and maintain their own privacy policies, terms of service, and data handling practises. The developer does not control and is not responsible for the privacy practises, availability, or content of third-party services.

Users are encouraged to review the policies of any third-party platforms they interact with through the bot.

7. Data Removal

Users and server administrators may request deletion of stored data at any time by removing the bot from a server or by contacting the developer directly.

Upon removal, associated data may be deleted automatically or through scheduled system processes. Some data may be retained temporarily where necessary for operational, security, or debugging purposes, but will not be kept longer than required for functionality.

Data removal is performed at the discretion of the developer or through automated systems where applicable.

Please note, as of June 25, 2026, there is no automated system to remove your data due to the recent change in the format of data.

8. Security

The developer uses reasonable technical and organizational security measures to help protect stored data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures may include industry-standard practices such as access restrictions, secure hosting configurations, and protective system controls commonly used for applications of this type.

While these measures are designed to improve security, no system is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

This privacy policy, along with the Terms of Service, may be updated, modified, or replaced at any time to reflect changes in the bot, its features, or its data handling practices.

When changes are made, the updated policy will take effect immediately upon being published or updated in its designated location. Continued use of the bot after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
